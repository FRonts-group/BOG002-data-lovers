How to play

Game Instructions

You have to guess which pokemon has a greater stat in one of the following stats:

  • Health Points
  • Attack Points
  • Defense Points
  • Special Attack Points
  • Special Defense Points
  • Speed Points

You see the stat to be compared under the buttons with the name of each pokemon.

Then you have to choose which one has the greater stat with the buttons.

Your score increases if you get a correct answer.
